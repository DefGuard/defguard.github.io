#### 1. **Service Provider**

The data controller is **Teonite Services spółka z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością**, with its registered office in Szczecin, Poland, at ul. Cyfrowa 6/317, 71-441 Szczecin. The company can be contacted at **support@defguard.net**.

#### 2. **Collected Data**

We collect the following personal data:

- **Email address**: Provided during purchase for license delivery and invoicing.
- **Billing information**: Necessary to issue invoices.
- **Support inquiries**: Information provided during communication with our support team.

We do not process or store payment details, which are handled by the payment processor.

#### 3. **Purpose of Data Processing**

Your data is processed for:

- Fulfilling contractual obligations, including license delivery and support.
- Issuing invoices.
- Ensuring proper service functionality.
- Responding to support requests.

#### 4. **Cookies Usage**

We use cookies to improve user experience and for analytics purposes. This includes:

- **Essential cookies**: Necessary for the website's operation.
- **Google Analytics cookies**: To collect anonymous data on website usage, such as:
  - User interactions (page views, clicks).
  - Device and browser information.
  - Geographical location (approximate).
  - Traffic sources (referring websites).

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. For more information about Google Analytics data collection, visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

#### 5. **Data Retention**

- Billing and transaction data: Retained for legal and tax purposes for 5 years.
- Support data: Retained for 2 years after the last interaction.
- Google Analytics data: Retained as per Google's standard policies (currently up to 26 months).

#### 6. **Data Sharing**

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except:

- Payment processors for payment verification.
- Legal authorities when required by law.
- Google Analytics (anonymous usage data).

#### 7. **Your Rights**

You have the right to:

- Access your data.
- Correct or delete your data.
- Restrict data processing.
- Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies.

To exercise your rights, contact **support@defguard.net**.

#### 8. **Security Measures**

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

#### 9. **Changes to the Privacy Policy**

Changes to this Privacy Policy take effect 24 hours after publication.

#### 10. **Governing Law**

This Privacy Policy is governed by Polish law. Any disputes will be resolved in the courts competent for the service provider's registered office (Szczecin, Poland).
